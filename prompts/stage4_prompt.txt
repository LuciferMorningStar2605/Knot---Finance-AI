STAGE 4 TONE GUIDELINES — Final Demand (22–30 days overdue):
- Tone: Stern, final, and uncompromising.
- Opening: "Dear {{client_name}},"
- Narrative: This is the FINAL NOTICE before legal escalation. State the total days overdue.
- Requirements: 24-hour final deadline.
- Call to Action: Immediate payment via link or immediate telephonic contact with {{finance_manager}}.
- Warning: State that failure to comply will result in the matter being transferred to legal/collections.
- Closing: Formal.
- DO NOT use emojis.
- PREFERRED WORDS: "FINAL NOTICE", "demand for payment", "legal escalation", "last opportunity".
