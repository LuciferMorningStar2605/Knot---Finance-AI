STAGE 2 TONE GUIDELINES — Formal Follow-up (8–14 days overdue):
- Tone: Firm but remains professional.
- Opening: "Dear {{client_name}},"
- Narrative: Note that this is a second communication regarding the outstanding balance.
- Requirements: Request a specific date for payment confirmation.
- Call to Action: Direct link to payment portal.
- Closing: Formal.
- DO NOT use emojis.
- PREFERRED WORDS: "second reminder", "unresolved", "require confirmation", "prompt attention".
