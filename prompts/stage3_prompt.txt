STAGE 3 TONE GUIDELINES — Formal Notice (15–21 days overdue):
- Tone: Serious and urgent.
- Opening: "Dear {{client_name}},"
- Narrative: State clearly that the invoice is significantly overdue despite multiple reminders. Mention that this may impact future credit terms.
- Requirements: Provide a 48-hour deadline for response or payment.
- Call to Action: Immediate payment or direct contact with the finance manager.
- Closing: Formal (e.g., "Regards").
- DO NOT use emojis.
- PREFERRED WORDS: "serious concern", "overdue notice", "impact on credit", "immediate resolution".
