STAGE 1 TONE GUIDELINES — Formal Reminder (1–7 days overdue):
- Tone: Professional, courteous, and helpful.
- Opening: Formal greeting. "Dear {{client_name}},"
- Narrative: Assume a potential administrative oversight. Mention that the invoice remains outstanding.
- Requirements: Include a "if payment has been initiated, please disregard" clause.
- Call to Action: Provide the payment link clearly.
- Closing: Professional sign-off (e.g., "Regards" or "Sincerely").
- DO NOT use emojis.
- DO NOT use casual language like "slip through the cracks".
- PREFERRED WORDS: "outstanding", "administrative reminder", "processed", "reference".
