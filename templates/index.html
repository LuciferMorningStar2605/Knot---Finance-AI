Knot is an autonomous, fail-safe credit collections platform designed for enterprise finance teams. It dramatically reduces DSO by analyzing delinquent invoices and generating context-aware follow-ups.

Architecture

Built to meet enterprise production standards, Knot replaces fragile, monolithic dashboards with a robust headless REST API architecture. It integrates a Smart-Retry LLM Engine to sanitize outputs and ensure zero hallucinations.

  • Flask, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL
  • Llama-3.3 70B & Mixtral 8x7B
  • Pydantic structured validation
  • Vanilla JS SPA + Tailwind CSS

Advantages

Designed for high-volume B2B collections, Knot allows finance managers to scale their recovery efforts without scaling their headcount.

  • 0% Failure Logic Gracefully handles LLM downtime and parsing errors without crashing.
  • Immutable Audit Trail Every action, legal flag, and AI generation is permanently logged.
  • Board-Ready Reports Instant PDF, Word, and highly-formatted Excel exports.
